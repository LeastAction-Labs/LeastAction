# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 2026**

> **Beta Notice:** LeastAction is currently in Beta. LeastAction Labs, Inc. operates as a small team and processes a minimal amount of personal data — primarily account and contact information collected through the leastactionlabs.com website and marketplace portal. LeastAction is **self-hosted software**: you deploy and operate it on your own infrastructure. LeastAction Labs does not host, access, or process any data within your LeastAction deployment.

## 1. Introduction and Scope

LeastAction Labs, Inc. ("LeastAction Labs," "we," "us," or "our"), a company incorporated under the Canada Business Corporations Act (R.S.C., 1985, c. This Privacy Policy applies to personal information we collect through:

- The leastactionlabs.com website, including its contact forms and documentation pages

**This Privacy Policy does NOT apply to data processed within your self-hosted LeastAction deployment.** When you deploy LeastAction on your own infrastructure, you are the **data controller** for all personal data processed by that deployment. LeastAction Labs has no access to, and assumes no responsibility for, data within your self-hosted instance. You are solely responsible for complying with applicable privacy laws in relation to your deployment.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you register for an account, subscribe to a plan, or contact us, we may collect:

- **Identity information:** First name, last name
- **Contact information:** Email address, organizational name, job title
- **Account credentials:** Email address and password (stored using bcrypt hashing); or OAuth/SSO token identifiers if you authenticate via a supported identity provider
- **Payment information:** Billing address, invoice details. Payment card details are processed directly by our third-party payment processor and are not stored on our systems.
- **Contact form submissions:** Any information you include when you contact us through the Website

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit the Website, we may automatically collect limited technical information:

- **Usage analytics:** Page views, referrer URL, browser type, operating system type (without persistent cross-site tracking or advertising profiling). We use privacy-respecting analytics tooling that does not build advertising profiles.
- **Server logs:** IP address, timestamp, HTTP method and path, response code. Logs are retained for a limited period for security and debugging purposes.
- **Cookies and similar technologies:** See our [Cookie Policy](/cookies) for details.

### 2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

- Data from your self-hosted LeastAction deployment
- Health, financial, or other sensitive personal categories beyond what is described above
- Personal information from children under 16

LeastAction Labs is a federally incorporated Canadian corporation (Canada Business Corporations Act, R.S.C., 1985, c. C-44) with its registered office in British Columbia. Our privacy practices are governed by:

- **BC PIPA (Personal Information Protection Act, SBC 2003, c. 63):** Applies to personal information we collect, use, and disclose in the course of commercial activities within British Columbia.
- **PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act, SC 2000, c. 5):** Applies to personal information we collect, use, or disclose in the course of interprovincial or international commercial activities.

Where the requirements of PIPA and PIPEDA overlap, we apply the more protective standard. Under BC PIPA and PIPEDA, you have the following rights regarding your personal information held by LeastAction Labs:

- **Right of correction:** You may request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [privacy@leastactionlabs.com](mailto:privacy@leastactionlabs.com). We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing requests.
